# Privacy Policy — Tertiary Scanner

_Last updated: 14 June 2026_

Tertiary Scanner ("the app") is published by Tertiary Infotech Pte Ltd ("we", "us"). This policy
explains how the app handles your information.

## Summary

**Tertiary Scanner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** Everything
the app does — scanning, image enhancement, text recognition (OCR), and PDF creation — runs
entirely on your device. The app has no analytics, no advertising, and no user accounts.

## Information we collect

**None.** We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an
account or sign in.

## Documents, images, and recognized text

- Documents you scan, the enhanced images, recognized text, and generated PDFs are stored
  **only on your device**, in the app's private storage.
- This content is never uploaded to us or to any third party.
- You remain in full control: you can delete any document at any time from within the app, and
  removing the app deletes all of its stored content.

## Permissions

- **Camera** (via the on-device document scanner) — used solely to capture documents. Images are
  processed on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.
- **Photos / media access** — used only to import images you choose, or to save images and PDFs
  you explicitly export.

The app does not request internet/network access for the purpose of collecting or sharing your
data.

## Third-party services

The app uses Google's on-device ML Kit libraries (document scanning and text recognition). These
run locally on your device and do not send your documents to Google or to us.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
date above.
