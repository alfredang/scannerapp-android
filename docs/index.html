Privacy Policy — Tertiary Scanner

Tertiary Infotech · Last updated 15 June 2026

Tertiary Scanner (“the app”), published by Tertiary Infotech Pte Ltd, is designed to be completely private. Everything the app does — scanning, image enhancement, text recognition (OCR), and PDF creation — runs entirely on your device.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no analytics, no advertising, and no user accounts, and does not require you to sign in.

Documents, images, and recognized text

  • Documents you scan, the enhanced images, recognized text, and generated PDFs are stored only on your device, in the app's private storage.
  • This content is never uploaded to us or to any third party.
  • You can delete any document at any time from within the app, and removing the app deletes all of its stored content.

Permissions

  • Camera (via the on-device document scanner) — used solely to capture documents. Images are processed on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.
  • Photos / media — used only to import images you choose, or to save images and PDFs you explicitly export.

The app does not request internet/network access to collect or share your data.

Third-party services

The app uses Google's on-device ML Kit libraries (document scanning and text recognition). These run locally on your device and do not send your documents to Google or to us.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
